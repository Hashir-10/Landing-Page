Ocean Of Oddities

The vast world of One-piece is goverened by a huge organisation, "World-Government". They fight for justice across the seas and aim to bring peace into the world.... or do they? This organisation holds many dark secrets hidden from common eyes.

The 4 unshakable titans also known as "Yonkos" who rule the seas with a might that even the World-Government thinks twice before engaging with them on any matter. There names are : "Red-hair Shanks", "Straw-hat Luffy", "Blackbeard(Marshall.D.Teach)". "Buggy the clown".

In the vast world of One-piece there are many mysteries, one of which are ancient-weapons. They are known as super destructive weapons from 800 years ago which can even destroy islands in a blink. As of now there is still not much information about them but their names are: "Pluton", "Uranus", "Poseidon".

In One-piece there are mysterious fruits that when eaten give you powers of different kind but on the cost of your swimming ability. There are 4 types of devil fruit in One-piece: "Paramecia", "Logia", "Zoan", "God-fruits".

The One-piece world also has various creatures around the seas. Some can be extremely dangerous and life threatening while others are calm and friendly.

To navigate through this vast ocean of mysteries an item called "Log-pose" is used, mainly at the grand-line. Each log-pose work differently, there are three main types: "Standard log-pose", "Eternal log-pose", "New-world log-pose".

Raftel or more accurately "Laugh-tale" is the last island of grand-line at the end of new-world. This is the island for which pirates are so eager to sail the seas and the island which holds many secrets of the world. It requires four road-poneglyphs to reach there. By far only one pirate crew has reached it "Gol.D.Rogers pirate crew". It will be ground breaking to see who reaches it next.
